📊 Visualization Homework

Chart 1: Average Square Footage by County

This bar chart shows the average square footage of buildings across different counties in Illinois. The x-axis represents the counties, sorted by average size, and the y-axis shows the mean square footage. A bar chart was chosen for easy comparison between categories. The tooltip reveals specific values on hover. No transformations were required apart from cleaning the column names.

Chart 2: Interactive Line Chart of Square Footage Over Time

This interactive line chart shows how average square footage has changed over time for the top 5 most common usage types (like “Assembly” or “Office”). Construction years are grouped into 5-year bins. Colors represent different usage types. A dropdown lets the user highlight one category at a time, and a hover effect adds focus on hover. The chart uses symmetric logarithmic scale for better comparison of values.
